LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE AGREEMENT (hereafter referred to as the "Agreement") made on April 21, 2023, by and between Jameson Property Management Limited, a company organized under the laws of England and Wales with its registered office at 45-47 High Street, Cobham, Surrey, KT11 3DP, United Kingdom (hereafter referred to as "Landlord") and Emma Thompson, an individual residing at 27 Queensgate Gardens, London, SW7 5JA, United Kingdom (hereafter referred to as "Tenant").

1. PREMISES: Landlord hereby leases to Tenant the premises located at 43-45 Thurloe Street, London, SW7 2LQ, United Kingdom (hereafter referred to as the "Premises").

2. TERM: The term of this Agreement shall begin on May 1, 2023, and end on April 30, 2024 (hereafter referred to as the "Term"). If Tenant wishes to renew this Agreement at the end of the Term, Tenant must notify Landlord in writing at least 60 days before the end of the Term.

3. RENT: Tenant shall pay rent in the amount of £3,500 per month, due on the 1st of each month. Rent shall be paid by direct debit to the account specified by Landlord. Tenant shall pay a late fee of £150 for any rent payment received after the due date.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT: Tenant shall pay a security deposit of £4,000 to Landlord on or before the start date of this Agreement. This deposit shall be used to cover any damages to the Premises caused by Tenant or Tenant's guests during the Term.

5. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS: Landlord shall be responsible for maintenance and repairs to the Premises, except for damages caused by Tenant or Tenant's guests. Tenant shall notify Landlord of any damages or needed repairs as soon as possible.

6. PETS: Pets are not allowed on the Premises unless otherwise approved by Landlord in writing. Any approved pets shall be subject to a non-refundable pet deposit of £500 and additional monthly rent of £150.

7. RENT INCREASE: Landlord may increase the rent amount at the end of the Term, provided that the increase is in line with the average rent increase in the local market.

8. RENEWAL: Landlord may choose to renew this Agreement at the end of the Term, provided that Tenant has not violated any of the terms of this Agreement. The renewal terms may be different from the terms of this Agreement.

9. LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales and any applicable federal laws.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between Landlord and Tenant and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements, whether written or oral. This Agreement may not be modified except in writing signed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
